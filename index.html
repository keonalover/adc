The Red-Flags Audit

What you get

Twenty-one days. An audit of your trailing twelve months of POS, labor, inventory, 3PO, and vendor invoice data (or the longest continuous period available). You get an explanation of overall business health, individual store performance, year-over-year trends, and long-term recommendations for the leakages found.

The Five Owner Questions

  1. Which locations are leaking profit?
  2. Where (labor, COGS, shrinkage)?
  3. Which location is performing the best?
  4. How can all locations perform like the best?
  5. How can these leaks be systematically monitored and prevented?
